
# Egg on toast

*makes one serving*

## Ingredients
- 1 egg
- butter
- bread

  ## Instructions
- put pan on fast boil on stove and set to 7
  - crack egg into bowl and stir
  - put butter on pan
  - pour egg into pan
  - put bread on top of egg
  - let cook then flip and let cook again
  - take off pan and put on plate
  - enjoy!
